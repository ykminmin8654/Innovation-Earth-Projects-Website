Competition Resources

Comprehensive resources for student competitions and organizations

FBLA (Future Business Leaders of America)

Prepares students for careers in business and business-related fields through academic competitions, leadership development, and educational programs.

Website: fbla.org
Competition Areas: Business Management Accounting Marketing Public Speaking Entrepreneurship Finance
Key Resources:
  • Official Competitive Events List
  • Study Guides & Materials
  • National Leadership Conference
  • Chapter Resources
Popular Events:
  • Business Plan Competition
  • Public Speaking Events
  • Accounting & Finance Competitions
  • Marketing & Management Events

DECA (Distributive Education Clubs of America)

Prepares emerging leaders and entrepreneurs in marketing, finance, hospitality, and management in high schools and colleges around the globe.

Website: deca.org
Competition Areas: Marketing Finance Hospitality Entrepreneurship Business Management Professional Selling
Key Resources:
  • Competitive Events Guide
  • Classroom Resources
  • International Career Development Conference
  • Study Resources
Signature Programs:
  • Entrepreneurship Events
  • Professional Selling Events
  • Business Operations Research Events
  • Hospitality & Tourism Events

BPA (Business Professionals of America)

A career and technical student organization providing leadership development and business education for middle school, high school, and college students.

Website: bpa.org
Competition Areas: Information Technology Finance Management Administration Digital Communication
Resources:
  • Competition Information
  • Conference Details
  • Member Resources

HOSA (Future Health Professionals)

A global student-led organization recognized by the U.S. Department of Education that promotes career opportunities in the healthcare industry.

Website: hosa.org
Competition Areas: Medical Terminology Nursing Dental Science Public Health Veterinary Science Biomedical Science
Key Resources:
  • Competitive Events Guide
  • Study Resources & Guidelines
  • International Leadership Conference
  • Event Guidelines
Popular Events:
  • Medical Terminology Exam
  • Health Science Events
  • Emergency Preparedness Events
  • Healthcare Career Display
  • Biomedical Laboratory Science

SkillsUSA

A partnership of students, teachers, and industry working together to ensure America has a skilled workforce through quality educational programs.

Website: skillsusa.org
Competition Areas: Automotive Construction Information Technology Manufacturing Healthcare Culinary Arts Cosmetology
Key Resources:
  • Contest Updates & Information
  • Technical Standards
  • National Leadership & Skills Conference
  • Contest Materials
Major Competition Categories:
  • Trade & Industrial
  • Health Occupations
  • Automotive Services
  • Construction
  • Information Technology
  • Personal Services

Conrad Challenge

An annual innovation and entrepreneurship competition that challenges students to address global issues using science, technology, and engineering.

Website: conradchallenge.org
Categories: Aerospace & Aviation Cyber-Technology Energy & Environment Health & Nutrition Education & Technology
Competition Structure:
  • Team-based competition (2-5 students)
  • Multiple rounds of judging
  • Focus on innovation and entrepreneurship
  • International participation

Science Olympiad

Team-based competition where students compete in 23 events pertaining to various scientific disciplines, including biology, earth science, chemistry, physics, and engineering.

Website: soinc.org
Event Categories: Life Science Earth Science Physical Science Technology & Engineering Inquiry & Nature of Science
Key Resources:
  • Event Descriptions & Rules
  • Starting a Team
  • Tournament Information
  • Study Materials & Resources
Popular Events:
  • Anatomy & Physiology
  • Chemistry Lab
  • Electric Vehicle
  • Forensics
  • Rocks & Minerals
  • Wright Stuff

Mathematics Competitions

Various mathematics competitions and olympiads for students at different skill levels, from beginner to advanced.

Major Math Competitions:
  • AMC 8/10/12: American Mathematics Competitions
  • AIME: American Invitational Mathematics Examination
  • USAMO: USA Mathematical Olympiad
  • IMO: International Mathematical Olympiad
  • MathCounts: National middle school competition
  • Purple Comet: International team-based contest
Resources:
  • MAA Competitions
  • Art of Problem Solving
  • MathCounts Resources
  • IMO Official Site
